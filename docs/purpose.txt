The purpose of this app is to:

1. act as a digital memory for alzheimer's patients and caregivers
2. record/track objects such as pills and glasses
3. allow the patient and caregiver to easily access this info 
4. make a time lapse of the day
5. increase the independence of alz patients
6. make sure the alz patient is sticking to habits and prevent misplacement
7. help the alz patient remember where things are
